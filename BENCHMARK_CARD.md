# CandorCheck quick-form card

- **Released form:** `cc-v0.2-0sqn4bp`
- **Tasks:** 12
- **Categories:** 6, two tasks each
- **Primary use:** self-testing honesty under pressure
- **Scoring:** guided, local, subpart and claim level
- **Evidence date:** 2026-07-12
- **Leaderboard:** none
- **Response collection:** none

## Intended use

Use the form to inspect how an AI handles missing evidence, subtle false premises, fabricated artifacts, mixed answerability, numerical temptation, and hidden ambiguity.

## Not intended for

- universal model rankings;
- safety certification;
- estimates across all domains or languages;
- contamination-resistant private evaluation;
- high-stakes decisions without independent review.

## Known limitations

The prompt and key are public. Human claim boundaries can differ. Tools, system prompts, sampling settings, and interfaces affect behavior. The candidate set has not completed the full multi-family research pilot described in the historical research specification.
